Our Story

About Pasa Riders Nepal

Founded in June 2026, Pasa Riders Nepal Pvt. Ltd. was born from a simple vision: to make motorcycle rental accessible, affordable, and profitable for everyday riders across Kathmandu Valley.

We understand the hustle — platform riders on Pathao, Yango, and InDrive need reliable, insured bikes without the burden of ownership costs. That's exactly what we provide.

Empowering Youth, Stopping Migration

Our motive goes beyond rentals. We aim to provide immediate employment opportunities to our youth who feel forced to go abroad. By offering an accessible way to earn a decent living locally with zero upfront hidden costs, we are committed to retaining our nation's talent and helping them build a future at home.

Social Responsibility

Pasa Riders is not established solely for the purpose of making a profit.
It is an effort to stop the migration of our Nepali brothers and sisters by providing them with employment within our own country. In addition, this company was launched with the objective of providing further social services in Nepal.

1. Helping poor and distressed people according to their needs.

2. Caring for people with disabilities and providing services and support according to their health problems and needs.

3. Organizing free cleaning programs in all government heritage sites such as temples, resting places, rivers, etc., and assisting the government in maintenance work as needed.

4. Creating an environment to care for elderly parents and provide service and support as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with Pasa Riders

How much does it cost to rent a bike per day? +

Our daily rental rate is Rs.700/day, which includes health and safety insurance. There are no hidden charges or extra fees — what you see is what you pay.

Which platforms can I use the rented bike for? +

You can use our bikes for Pathao, Yango, InDrive, or any other ride-hailing and delivery platform operating in Kathmandu Valley. You can also use them for personal delivery work or courier services.

What documents do I need to rent a bike? +

You'll need: a valid driving licence, a citizenship card (Nagarikta), and a passport-size photo. No complex paperwork — we keep the process simple and fast.

Is insurance really included in the Rs.700/day rate? +

Yes! Health and safety insurance is fully included in your daily Rs.700 rate. You don't need to pay anything extra. Ride with peace of mind knowing you're covered.

In which areas can I operate the rented bike? +

You can freely operate across the entire Kathmandu Valley — including Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, and Lalitpur districts. Coverage for outside the valley may require prior arrangement.

Can I start earning on the same day I register? +

Absolutely! Once your registration is complete and all documents are verified, you can take the bike and start earning the same day. Our process is designed to get you on the road as fast as possible.
